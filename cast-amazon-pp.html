Privacy Policy

Cast for Amazon does not centrally collect or store any personally identified or private information about individuals or phone state. Any information regarding phone network state, user credentials or identity are used solely for local application functionality and are not sent to or stored by Night Time Industries LLC or any third parties except Amazon and its affliates for the direct purpose of authentication and management of video services, such as, but not limited to viewing history, playback location and watchlists.

Non identifiable statistics, metrics and crash reports may be collected to improve customer experience and app performance.
